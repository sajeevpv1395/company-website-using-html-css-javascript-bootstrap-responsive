Get Mtronzs Student Tracking App


Investing in a student tracking app is the need of the hour, especially now a days when parents are concerned about the safety and well being of the kids. Mtronz student Tracking App is the one-stop solution for any type of educational institutes to provide a channel of communication between the school staff and the students. Easy navigation User friendly dashboard Different panels for staff,parents and school admin. Well-structured reports Web,android and iOS applications with distinctive options.

Student Tracking App


web application

browser-based access to the Application from the Intranet (No External accesses initially) of the School/organization. Menu and items should be in accordance with the user authority.

Android Application

All users can log in to the mobile app irrespective of the roles specified. Menu and Items Should be According to the User authority. All options available in the web application will not be available in the mobile application.

IOS application

All users can log in to the mobile app irrespective of the roles specified. Menu and Items Should be According to the User authority. All options available in the web application will not be available in the mobile application

Student Tracking App Features


Admin Panel The school principal / management can act as the admin and control the overall working of the application. He/she can manage user accounts (parents, teachers and non teaching staff). The admin can also send notifications to the parents regarding the behaviour of the students, send SMS alerts and much more.

Parents Panel


Two members from the students family can have access to the app, the access being validated by an OPT at the earlier stage of the registration process. Parents will receive push notifications from the bus staff when their ward boards the bus. The attendance of the child will be sent to the parents from the teachers at regular intervals. Parents can track the location of the bus on the map from the details of the location logged in by the bus driver. The parents will have contact details of the class teacher, bus staff and other support staff.

Student Tracking App


Teacher Information

The App not only aids in availing information about the students but also stores the information regarding the teachers activities in school. Its easy to inspect a particular teachers class schedule file reports efficiently etc.

Transport Management

From our app it is easy to track student location if he she gets into the school bus. Through the mode of QR Code scanning of child s ID card parent can easily monitor where their child reached. Similarly the parents will get a quick notification if their child is about to reach home

Attendance Management

Attendance management is an essential school administrative task and it requires a lot of time for teachers to do the task at the beginning of each class. It is challenging for teachers to manage the whole process of taking attendance and reporting them accurately. Now, recording attendance of each student is just a click away.

Quick Notification

Our app provides timely feedback to the parents regarding the performance of the ward and helps build a cordial relationship between the school authorities and the children

Staff Panel


There is a separate panel for both the teaching staff and the non teaching staff. Teacher can send reports to the parents regarding the performance of the students and the conduct of the student. Real time updation of the attendance sheet of the students by the teachers are done. Non teaching staff includes the bus staff, who can log in the start of the bus and set the location. The parents can track where the bus has reached. There is also a provision for the parents to receive a text message 5 minutes prior to the bus reaching the bus stop. Similarly, when a student gets into the bus, there is a provision for scanning a QR code and the parents can know when the child reaches the school.
